About

Over his long career, Frank Hoedl has built a reputation as a visual storyteller. Thoughtful visual styling, attention to detail, and a deft approach to lighting are hallmarks of his work.

Behind the lens and as a retoucher, Frank has crafted many successful campaigns. In collaboration with many talented creative professionals, he has a solid foundation of great work.

As a result, he's won numerous awards at Cannes, the One Show, the Clios, the ADCC, the Marketing Awards and D&AD.

His work has been featured in Applied Arts, Strategy. Archive, Communication Arts Magazines, and Reader's Digest (Mom was proud).
